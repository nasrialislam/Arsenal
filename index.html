Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Highbury, London, England, that plays in the Premier League, the top flight of English football. In late 1886, a gaggle of workers from the Woolwich Arsenal Armament Factory decided to form a football team.
They called themselves Dial Square as a reference to the sundial atop the entrance to the factory.
